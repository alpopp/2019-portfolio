Hello, my name is Alex! I'm a developer and UX designer in Saint Louis. I live in South City with my wife, Ali, and our dog, Harry. When I'm not behind my desk, I'm probably hiking, enjoying a local brewery, doing some below-average woodworking, or running!

I currently serve as a senior web developer for Washington University in St. Louis. When I first started making websites, I had the opportunity to attend WordCamp St. Louis that was hosted on campus at WashU. I remember leaving the weekend having a tremendous ammount of respect for the work WashU does in WordPress, so to be able to work there now is really awesome and something I don't take for granted.
