ESCO Technologies wanted a redesign of their website, and wanted to use a CMS so they could keep their content up to date themselves. We worked with NASDAQ to develop the investor side of the website, since ESCO is a publically traded company.

ESCO often acquires other companies, so the website needed to be flexible and scaleable.
